Human Body Measurement

Developed an AI-powered contactless body measurement system using MediaPipe and computer vision to estimate clothing sizes accurately from images and videos. The system captures body dimensions, including shoulder width, chest, waist, and length, by analyzing front, side, and back views through mobile and desktop cameras. Optimized algorithms ensure precise size recommendations, eliminating the need for physical measurements.
